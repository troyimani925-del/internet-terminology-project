Definition

An extranet is an extension of an intranet that allows limited access to external users such as partners, suppliers, or clients. It provides controlled access to specific resources within an organization’s network.

It is designed to facilitate collaboration between an organization and external stakeholders.

Explanation

Through an extranet, authorized external users can access certain data or services without being granted full access to the internal network.

This improves communication and efficiency in business operations while maintaining security and control over sensitive information.
